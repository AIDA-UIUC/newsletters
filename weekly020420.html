SIG AI/Data Analytics Weekly Newsletter



For those new to AIDA, welcome! If you missed the info session, here are the slides.

All events will be taking place in Siebel 1105.

Wednesday (2/5)

  • 5-7PM: AI Workshop - Intro to Numpy
    • Come join us to learn the basics of numpy, the "fundamental package for computing" in Python. Numpy is the basis for fast number manipulations in Python, and will be increasingly important to understand dealing with AI algorithms.

Thursday (2/6)

  • 5-7PM: AI Research
    • Discuss current research in AI! An informal session dedicated to the quickly changing field.

Sunday (2/8)

  • 2-3PM: DS Workshop - Data Cleansing with Pandas
    • Now that we've gotten Python basics out of the way, let's start doing some analyses! The first step in any analysis is to gather and clean data. We'll work on our knowledge of arrays in order to "clean" (prepare for analysis) a small data set! If you missed our last meeting on the basics of Python, the workbook is available here.
  • 3-4PM: Projects
    • The best way to learn data science is to practice! Projects meetings are unstructured meetings that exist solely for you to find other people with similar interests! Consult the Slack channel for a current list of proposed projects.

Events and Opportunities

As always, if you want to help us out, please feel free to contact us. For a list of events that might interest you, visit our Slack channel (#sigaida)!
